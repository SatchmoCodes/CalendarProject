YOU MIGHT BE A BAD PARENT

(Daily Inspirational Calendar)

January 1, 2022

LEIF ERIKSON'S DAY

If in absence of any formal diagnosis, you claim that your tenth-grade son has a medical condition that requires him to be in direct sunlight the entire school day and your expectation is that the school alter the schedules of all of the teachers and students so that your son can stay in the same room all day, you might be a bad parent.
